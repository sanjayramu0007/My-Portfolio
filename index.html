On-page SEO

On-page SEO is the process of improving the structure and content-like text,images and videos of webpages.Placing a target keywords,accurate titls tags and meta descriptions, Optimizing your URL.

Technical SEO

Technical SEO is the process of optimizing the "behind-the-scenes" foundation of your website.It ensure that search engines can easily find,understand,and organize your pages.It optimizes your site's backend infrastructure so search engines can easily crawl,understand and index your pages.

Off-page SEO

Off-page SEO refers to all action taken outside of your own website-such as earning backlinks,digital PR, and brand mentions to influnce your rankings on search engine result pages. It builds authority,reputation and visibility outside the site.

Link Building

Link Building is the process of earning hyperlinks from other external websites to your owen. Thesea incoming links(called backlink). Search engines like google use them to measure a site's authority and relevence, making them a crucial factor for ranking higher in search results.
